# Revenue Operations & Customer Churn Analysis
## ggsocials Strategy Case Study

### Executive Summary
This project analyzes a dataset of over 7,000 customers to identify revenue leakage and retention of key metrics.

![Dashboard Preview](dashboard.png)

### Key Insights
1. **The 10-Month Wall:** 20% of clients churn within the first 10 months.
2. **The Engagement "Death Zone":** Inactivity beyond **70-80 days** results in a 25% spike in case churn.
3. **The Veteran Anchor:** Clients with 50+ months tenure represent **$3.1M** (approx. 30%) of total cases.
